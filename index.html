Nanner Scanner

A distance calculator with a universal unit of measurement

Like the cubit of the Pharoahs, the foundation of all great civilizations is a universal form of measurement. Today, our greatest monument floats in the stars, a space station that supercedes the confines of nationalism for a unified pursuit of heaven's mysteries. As humanity stumbles towards the stars, The Nanner Scanner hopes to highlight both the magnificence of our planet's size and its orbital crown jewel with an implement accessible to all:
"Banana for scale."
